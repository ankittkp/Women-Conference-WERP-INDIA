The Women Empowerement Conference

Powered By WERP India

25 December, 2018 - 30 December, 2018
Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium, Delhi
The Women Empowerment Conference held by Women Empowerment Research Project (WERP) India is taking place for the first time in India. This will internationally provide a bigger platform for generating answers to the wide-known and problematic questions by facilitating interactive session among the stakeholders, women, men, Entrepreneurs/ business organizations, non-governmental organizations, government organizations, research and academia, policy makers, as well as students.
This integrating, multiple stakeholder conference for womenâ€™s empowerment will look for integrated solutions and recommendations for solving the problems of women and the atrocities they face in India which can further up-scaled globally for sustainable and better future of the world.
The conference is about improving the status of women and how we expect our future India to be.This conference is to bring change in the society and as well as the women who are still lagging themselves behind the bars of their houses and to those who are still getting burned in fire of the kitchen. It's not just a conference, it's a vision of a new world where a women need not struggle for empowerment. It's a platform where each and every women is welcomed and not restricted to show her talent , to express herself and to share her stories and her experiences that can inspire and aspire the millions of women to break those traditional barriers that tied her up . Our conference is to serve as a voice for those whose voices have been crushed under the feet of societal aspects of life. This conference can provide a light for the women surrounded in the darkness.
